Demo checklist:
1. Start MySQL, backend, ML API, and Ollama.
2. Create one patient for HIGH risk and add three severe records.
3. Create one patient for LOW risk and add three mild records.
4. Show /risk and /explanation responses.
5. Set ENABLE_ML=false in .env, restart backend, and show rule-based fallback.
6. Set ENABLE_LLM=false in .env, restart backend, and show static explanation fallback.
7. Change WEIGHT_SEVERITY or WEIGHT_DURATION, restart backend, and show changed score.
